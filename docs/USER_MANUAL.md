# Patient User Manual

A quick guide to using AI'll Be Sick, your AI-powered symptom checker.

> **Important:** This app is a research tool, not a replacement for professional medical care. Always consult a healthcare provider for medical concerns.

---

## Getting Started

When you first log in, you'll need to accept the terms and complete a short onboarding guide. This only happens once.

**Sidebar Navigation:**
- **New Chat** - Start a symptom check
- **History** - View past diagnoses
- **Help** - Reopen the app guide
- **Sign Out** - Log out

---

## New Diagnosis

Click **New Chat** to start checking your symptoms.

**Two ways to enter symptoms:**

1. **Type freely** - Describe how you feel in the text box (e.g., "I have a headache and fever for three days"). Press Enter or click the arrow to submit.

2. **Use the checklist** - Click the clipboard icon to select symptoms from organized categories. You can switch between English and Filipino using the globe icon. Select at least 2 symptoms, then submit.

**Follow-up questions:** The AI may ask Yes/No questions to better understand your symptoms. Answer honestly for accurate results.

---

## Your Results

After analysis, you'll see a diagnosis card with a suggested condition.

**Confidence levels:**
| Level | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Strong match** (95%+) | Symptoms closely match this condition |
| **Possible match** (70-94%) | Partial match, some uncertainty |
| **Weak match** (<70%) | Weak overlap, other conditions possible |

**Actions:**
- **View Insights** - See which symptoms influenced the result
- **Record Diagnosis** - Save to your history
- **Discard Diagnosis** - Remove without saving

---

## History

Click **History** to view all your past diagnoses.

**Features:**
- **Search** - Find specific diagnoses by keyword
- **Sort** - By date, diagnosis name, confidence, or uncertainty
- **Filter** - Show only specific conditions
- **Export PDF** - Download your history
- **Delete** - Remove unwanted records (click trash icon)

Click the link icon on any entry to view full details.

---

## Profile

Access your profile through the sidebar to manage your information.

**Personal info:** Update your name, avatar, gender, and birthday.

**Location:** Select your district/zone (required). You can also search for your address or click "Use my location" to detect it automatically.

---

## Need Help?

- Click the **Help** button in the sidebar to reopen the app guide
- For technical issues, contact your healthcare facility

---

*Remember: Always consult a healthcare professional for medical advice.*
